Pacman

Enjoy the classic Pacman arcade game! Eat all the dots, avoid the ghosts, and chase the high score.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move Pacman around the maze.
  • Eat all the dots to complete the level.
  • Avoid the ghosts or eat power pellets to turn the tables!
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible!
